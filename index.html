Kiko Milano

Kiko Milano is all about celebrating individuality through self-expression. They draw inspiration from global trends but add their unique Italian flair to create high-quality products that encourage people to embrace their uniqueness. Their products are all designed in Milano, with 99% of products produced in Europe to the highest quality standards.

With a wide range of 1,200 products in various shades, designed and produced with excellence in Europe, Kiko Milano has become a leading beauty brand in Italy, renowned for its prestigious reputation across Europe and all over the world.

Rituals...

Rituals is a cosmetic brand that believes in finding joy in life's little moments. They're all about turning everyday routines into something special. Like artists, they carefully select natural ingredients to create products with their own unique charm.

They're serious about sustainability, too. Rituals proudly holds a B Corp™ certification, which means they meet high standards for social and environmental responsibility. They're also committed to achieving Net-Zero emissions by 2050, showing their dedication to combating climate change.

In short, Rituals is all about making you feel good while doing good for the planet.

Victoria's Secret

Victoria's Secret is a renowned lingerie and beauty brand known for its glamorous and seductive aesthetic.Their beauty offerings often embody the brand's sensual and playful aesthetic, with products designed to make customers feel glamorous and confident.

With a focus on empowering confidence and femininity, Victoria's Secret offers a wide range of lingerie, sleepwear, and beauty products. Their lingerie collections are synonymous with luxury and sophistication, featuring intricate lace details, flattering cuts, and bold designs that cater to diverse body types.

Overall, Victoria's Secret is a brand that celebrates femininity, sensuality, and self-expression, offering products that empower individuals to feel confident and beautiful in their own skin.

e.l.f. Cosmetics

e.l.f. Cosmetics, or Eyes Lips Face, is a makeup brand that believes beauty should be inclusive, original, and ethical. They offer a wide range of products for every makeup style, from bold to natural. Their products are 100% vegan and cruelty-free, affirming their dedication to animal welfare and responsible manufacturing processes.

Despite offering high-quality products, e.l.f. maintains accessible price points, making beauty accessible to all without compromising on ethics or performance.

In essence, e.l.f. empowers individuals to express themselves authentically through makeup, offering a guilt-free beauty experience that celebrates diversity and creativity.

ColourPop

ColourPop is a vibrant and innovative cosmetics brand known for its high-quality products at affordable prices. With a focus on inclusivity and creativity, ColourPop offers a diverse range of makeup options suitable for all skin tones and preferences.

A wide range of colorful eyeshadows, beautiful lipsticks, brilliant highlighters, and flexible face palettes are all part of their product portfolio.ColourPop also offers skincare products, such as facial cleansers, moisturizers, and masks, designed to enhance natural beauty and promote healthy skin..

ColourPop is a brand that celebrates individuality, self-expression, and inclusivity, providing customers with the tools and inspiration to experiment with makeup and express their unique personalities
